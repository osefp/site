About

Our Mission

The Open Social Engineering Framework Project (OSEFP) is a non-profit, community-driven initiative dedicated to making social engineering security knowledge accessible, visible, and actionable for everyone.

Inspired by OWASP's model for application security and the Social-Engineer.org Framework's deep focus on human-layer vulnerabilities, OSEFP provides the comprehensive knowledge base that bridges technical security and human psychology.

Our framework equips security professionals, penetration testers, researchers, and organizations with the methodologies, tools, and knowledge to understand, test, and defend against social engineering attacks.

Core Values

Open & Free — All resources available under Creative Commons licenses
Community Driven — Built by volunteers, for the global security community
Ethical Practice — Responsible use and professional conduct at all times
Education First — Empowering defenders through deep understanding
Vendor Neutral — Independent, unbiased, and commercially impartial
